How It Works
  1. Select multiple images from your computer
  2. Images will be uploaded to imgur for free hosting
  3. Each image will retain its original filename as the title
  4. A PDF will be generated with:
    • The image displayed
    • The image title (filename)
    • The imgur hosted URL (permanent link)
  5. Download the PDF with all image information
Images are hosted anonymously on imgur and will have permanent URLs that you can use anywhere.
